Overview

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private research deemed university located in Katpadi in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. VIT Vellore, a prestigious Indian institute, is known for its world-class education in engineering, technology, and management. Established in 1984, VIT has consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions in India. The campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, innovative research, and a vibrant student community, making it a renowned educational hub. The institution offers 66 Undergraduate, 58 Postgraduate, 15 Integrated, 2 Research and 2 M.Tech Industrial Programmes. It has campuses in Vellore and Chennai and sister universities in Amaravati, Bhopal and Bengaluru. Known for its rigorous academic curriculum, VIT emphasizes research, entrepreneurship, and industry collaboration. The campus features modern infrastructure, international collaborations, and a strong focus on holistic development, producing skilled and globally competitive graduates.VIT Vellore is renowned for its strong emphasis on innovation and technology. The institute hosts various national and international conferences, fostering an environment conducive to cutting-edge research. With a commitment to holistic education, VIT Vellore provides students with opportunities for cultural, sports, and extracurricular activities, creating well-rounded individuals.
